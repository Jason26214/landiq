# 5. Infrastructure

## 5.1 Water Supply 「MANUAL」

**Why it matters:** Must confirm capacity for proposed development. Larger developments may need main extensions/amplifications.

**Where to find:** Sydney Water "Tap in", Melbourne Water + retail corps, regional utilities.

**Red flags:** No existing main nearby, insufficient capacity (amplification at developer cost), water pressure issues on elevated sites, greenfield headworks $10k-$50k+/lot.

**Cost:** Feasibility advice: $0-$500. Section 73: $1,000-$5,000. Headworks: $5,000-$50,000+/lot.

---

## 5.2 Sewer 「MANUAL」

**Why it matters:** Often the binding infrastructure constraint. Amplification is expensive. No sewer = on-site treatment (may not support density).

**Red flags:** No reticulated sewer, main at capacity (amplification $50k-$500k+), sewer traversing site (easement), pump station needed.

**Cost:** Plan search: $30-$100. Developer charges: $5,000-$50,000+/lot.

---

## 5.3 Electricity 「MANUAL」

**Why it matters:** Larger developments may need transformer upgrades or new substations at developer cost.

**Where to find:** Distributor (Ausgrid, Endeavour, AusNet, CitiPower, etc.).

**Red flags:** No nearby HV supply (new substation $500k-$2M+), transformer at limit, HV overhead lines traversing site (easement + EMF setbacks).

**Cost:** Connection inquiry: $0-$500. Supply charges: $5,000-$100,000+/lot.

---

## 5.4 Gas 「MANUAL」

**Why it matters:** Increasingly optional as developments go all-electric. Gas easement may restrict building.

**Cost:** $0 (network check). Connection: $2,000-$10,000/dwelling.

---

## 5.5 Telecommunications (NBN) 「HYBRID」

**Why it matters:** All new developments must connect to NBN. Check availability and technology type.

**Where to find:** nbnco.com.au/connect-home-or-business/check-your-address

**Cost:** $0 (address check). Conduit installation: $1,000-$5,000/lot.

---

## 5.6 Stormwater Drainage 「MANUAL」

**Why it matters:** Must manage increased runoff (WSUD). Council may require on-site detention (OSD).

**Red flags:** No existing infrastructure to connect, site is natural drainage depression, overland flow path through site.

**Cost:** Concept design: $3,000-$10,000. Detailed design: $5,000-$30,000.

---

## 5.7 Road Access 「MANUAL」

**Why it matters:** Must have legal access from public road. Larger developments trigger traffic assessment and state road authority referral.

**Red flags:** Landlocked parcel, access from classified road (state authority may refuse), intersection at capacity (upgrade $100k-$2M+).

**Cost:** $0 (initial). Traffic assessment: $5,000-$25,000.

---

## 5.8 Public Transport Proximity 「API-AUTO」

**Why it matters:** Affects density allowances, parking rates, market appeal. TOD SEPP (NSW) applies within 400m-1.2km of rail stations.

**API:** GTFS open data — TfNSW, PTV VIC. Distance calculation trivially automatable.

**Cost:** $0 (free open data).
